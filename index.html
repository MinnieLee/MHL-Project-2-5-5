Q5.

What is your favorite artwork?

A5.

Scale

My favorite artwork is Scale. I like the message of this artwork. Through this artwork I wanted to express the weight of invisible things. It seems that our life is more influenced by invisible things. Stress does not have any shape, however, it weighs down our life. I wanted to awaken people to the power of what is invisible, things that are invisible or ungraspable.
